Crimes through the eyes of a team of forensic pathologists and forensic scientists

Silent Witness is a BBC crime thriller series focusing on a team of forensic pathology experts and their investigations into various crimes.

The Origin

The original series was based on Professor Helen Witwell, a forensic pathologist based in Sheffield whom McCrery had known while serving as a police officer.

Main Characters

  • Nikki Alexander (Emilia Fox) – since Series 8
  • Jack Hodgson (David Caves) – since Series 16
  • Clarissa Mullery (Liz Carr) – Series 16–23
  • Thomas Chamberlain (Richard Lintern)– Series 17–23

Silencium

The theme music featured in the series is entitled Silencium and is performed by John Harle.The vocal section is performed by Sarah Leonard.

Post Mortem

One of the most distinctive features of Silent Witness is its focus on the post mortem process. The pathologists spend much of their time in the Lyell Centre's laboratory, so a huge amount of effort is spent ensuring this aspect of the series is as intriguing and authentic as possible.

Best TV Drama at European Science TV and New Media Awards

Silent Witness: Legacy, a two-part story written by Steven Gallagher for series 16 of the show, has won the award for Best TV Drama at The European Science TV and New Media Awards 2013.

Silent Witness will return

The BBC has announced that Silent Witness will return for a further two series - a 24th as well as the 25th Anniversary series. Piers Wenger, Controller of BBC Drama, says: "Silent Witness entertains audiences from all generations and continues to be one of the UK’s most successful returning crime drama series.
